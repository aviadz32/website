Aviad Zinemanas is a vinyl-driven DJ whose sets travel across continents. Known for weaving global rhythms into a seamless musical narrative, he blends everything from Colombian cumbia and Japanese city pop to Obscure disco, other rare international cuts found thourgh his travels. His selections balance deep-crate discoveries with dance-floor intuition.

Drawing from years of experience as a musician and producer, Aviad approaches every set with an ear for mood and texture. His background in composition adds a cinematic quality to his mixes, allowing him to move fluidly between genres while maintaining a distinctive sonic identity.
