Sound Design & Original Music

Press Start is a six-part docu-series, each episode following a different story around the world of gaming. The series was aired on the Israeli public broadcaster digital channels and is available to watch on YouTube.

Original Music

Patrick is a thriller TV show about Patrick, a crime boss who survives an assassination attempt but loses his memory. He wakes up with no knowledge of his previous life, trying to navigate the world of crime he was once part of. The music for the show was produced in collaboration with the composer Shushan and is available to stream on Channel 13's website.
