Amor (2019) is an audiovisual composition made in collaboration with Artist Dor Zelkha Levy. Amor is the artistsâ€™ personal interpretation of the emotional state that is referred to in Portuguese as Saudade, a longing for an absent someone or something that one cares for and loves.

Amor was first displayed in 2019 at ROOM25, a contemporary art gallery in Tel Aviv. The work was accessible at all hours via code and allowed viewers a completely private experience.
