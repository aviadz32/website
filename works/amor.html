Amor is an audiovisual composition made in collaboration with Artist Dor Zelkha Levy. Amor is the artists’ personal interpretation of the emotional state that is referred to in Portuguese as Saudade– a longing for an absent someone or something that one cares for and loves. The video work focuses on fragments of human expressions and gestures. Several-seconds film fragments have been vertically cropped and heavily slowed down until they dismantled from their specific identity and bleed into color and motion.

Frame from Amor

Amor was first displayed in 2019 at ROOM25, a contemporary art gallery in Tel Aviv. The work was accessible at all hours via code and allowed viewers a completely private experience. Since then it has also taken form as an online work for mobile devices.
